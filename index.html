About Us

Charis Farm, situated in Naivasha, stands as the pioneering farmer's demonstration center in Nakuru County and its surroundings.

This innovative farm provides training in a spectrum of modernized and simplified farming practices, encompassing dairy goat and rabbit rearing, ornamental bird keeping, black soldier fly cultivation, pig farming, and aquaculture.

Moreover, Charis Farm offers expertise in kitchen gardening, azolla farming, agri-tourism, local tourism, and more, all tailored to enhance smart farming practices, particularly maximizing productivity within limited space.

The farm boasts comprehensive training facilities, including internet access, and a fully furnished three-bedroom holiday home, ensuring a comfortable and enriching learning environment..

Our Services

With our services, we aim to empower farmers and the community with the knowledge and skills needed to improve their agricultural practices and increase their productivity and income.

Training

Smart Farming

Charis Farm is at the forefront of smart farming practices, specializing in maximizing productivity within constrained spaces.

Animal Rearing

Educational Tours

Experience agriculture firsthand with Charis Farm's educational tours. Learn about modern farming practices, interact with our animals, and explore sustainable farming methods, all while enjoying the serene environment of our farm.

Farm Produce

Charis Farm offers a variety of organic farm-fresh produce for sale. From vegetables grown in our kitchen gardens to dairy products from our goats, our produce is of the highest quality, reflecting our commitment to sustainable and ethical farming practices.

Fish Farming

Charis Farm specializes in home economical fish farming, offering comprehensive training and support for individuals interested in this sustainable practice. Our fish farming program covers everything from setting up a fish pond to breeding and feeding techniques.

Small Space Utilization

Beauty in our Birds

Charis Farm offers a diverse range of services, including the rearing and sale of ornamental birds. Our collection includes a variety of colorful and exotic bird species, perfect for adding beauty and charm to any farm or home. such as;

  • Bantam (Polish, Silk, Bearded booted)
  • Chickens (Brahma, Kuchi, Ameraucana, Ayam Cemani, frizzle, Sweedish flower)
  • Peacocks
  • Turkeys (Lavender white, Bourbon)
  • Fowls (Dotted Guinea, pure White Guinea, Lavender Guinea, Vulterine Guinea, Royal Purple, White chested)
  • Love Birds
  • Indian Funtails
  • Capuchin Pegions
  • Geese (Chinese, Local, Egyptian)
  • Ducks (Peckin, Rouen, Indian Runners, Muscovy)

Beyond their beauty, some of our ornamental birds possess unique abilities. They can be used to predict the weather and disasters, making them valuable assets to any farm. These birds are also resistant to diseases, require less food, and have longer lifespans compared to other bird species. By integrating these birds into their operations, farmers can benefit from their unique abilities and characteristics, contributing to a more sustainable and profitable farming experience.

High Breed Rearing

Charis Farm specializes in high breed rearing of goats, pigs, and rabbits, providing expert guidance and support for farmers interested in these endeavors.

  • Goat Rearing: Our goat rearing program focuses on breeds known for their high milk production. We provide training on breeding techniques, feeding practices, housing, and disease and pest management, ensuring farmers can maintain healthy and productive goat herds.
  • Pig Rearing: Charis Farm excels in high breed pig rearing, emphasizing breeds known for superior meat quality and growth rates. Our training covers breeding, housing, feeding, and disease and pest prevention, enabling farmers to practice optimal pig farming techniques.
  • Rabbit Rearing: We offer expert guidance on high breed rabbit rearing, focusing on breeds valued for their meat and fur quality. Our training includes breeding techniques, housing, feeding, and disease management, ensuring successful rabbit farming practices..

Overall, Charis Farm's high breed rearing programs are designed to help farmers achieve optimal results in goat, pig, and rabbit farming. We aim to ensure healthy livestock and profitable farming ventures for all our farmers.

Exploring Agricultural Education: A Visit from Gituamba High School to Charis Farm

Julia Owino
/
School Visits

Last week, Charis Farm had the pleasure of hosting a group of students and teachers from Maraigushu High School on a visit to our farm.

The visit was an opportunity for the students to learn about modern farming practices and gain practical knowledge that they can apply in their own agricultural endeavors. The day began with a warm welcome from our team, who introduced the students to the various aspects of our farm. We started with a tour of our facilities, including our training halls, internet-equipped library, and fully furnished holiday home. The students were impressed by the resources available to them and eager to learn more about our farming techniques. One of the highlights of the visit was our demonstration of smart farming practices. We showed the students how we maximize productivity in limited spaces using vertical, multi-story, keyhole, tire, sack, and Moisty Moisty raised gardening techniques. The students were fascinated by these innovative methods and asked many questions about how they could implement similar practices at their school. We also introduced the students to our high breed rearing programs, focusing on goats, pigs, and rabbits. We explained the breeding techniques, feeding practices, housing requirements, and disease management strategies that are essential for successful livestock farming. The students were particularly interested in our rabbit rearing program, as they had never seen such high-quality breeds before. Throughout the visit, our team emphasized the importance of sustainable farming practices and the role that young farmers like the students from Gituamba High School can play in promoting food security and environmental conservation. We encouraged them to share what they had learned with their classmates and to consider pursuing careers in agriculture. As the day came to a close, we thanked the students and teachers from Maraigushu High School for visiting us and expressed our hope that they had found the experience both educational and inspiring. We look forward to welcoming more schools to Charis Farm in the future and continuing to promote agricultural education and innovation in our community.

Community Empowerment

Charis Farm recently welcomed a group of enthusiastic farmers eager to learn and explore innovative farming practices.

Hailing from various regions, these farmers embarked on a journey to Charis Farm in search of new techniques and insights to enhance their agricultural endeavors.
